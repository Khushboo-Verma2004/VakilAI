About Us

Discover the story behind VakilAI, your trusted AI-powered legal assistant, and how we’re revolutionizing legal access in India.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, VakilAI was born out of a simple observation: legal documents are unnecessarily complex and inaccessible to most Indians. Our team of legal experts and AI engineers came together to bridge this gap. What started as a small project to analyze rental agreements has now grown into a comprehensive platform supporting 22 Indian languages and all major document types. Today, we serve thousands of users across India, helping them understand their legal rights and obligations with clarity and confidence.

Our Mission

To empower every Indian with instant, accurate, and understandable legal analysis in their native language, breaking down barriers to legal access and ensuring no one signs a document they don’t fully understand.
